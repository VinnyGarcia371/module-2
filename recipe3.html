IngredientsDirections
2 teaspoons olive oil Position one oven rack in the middle setting. Position another rack in the lowest setting, and place a rimless baking sheet on the bottom rack. Preheat oven to 500°.
1 cup fat-free bottled pizza sauce Remove plastic wrap from Basic Pizza Dough; discard plastic. Brush olive oil over dough. Remove preheated baking sheet from oven, and close oven door. Slide dough onto preheated baking sheet, using a spatula as a guide. Bake on lowest oven rack at 500° for 8 minutes. Remove from oven.
2 cups (8 ounces) shredded part-skim mozzarella cheese Spread sauce in an even layer over crust, leaving a 1/4-inch border. Top with mozzarella and Parmesan cheeses. Bake on middle rack an additional 10 minutes or until crust is golden brown and cheese melts. Cut into 12 wedges.
1/2 cup (2 ounces) grated fresh Parmesan cheese

Supreme Pizza

Click here





IngredientsDirections
1 (16-ounce) refrigerated fresh pizza crust dough Preheat oven to 500°.
Cooking spray Roll dough into a 14-inch circle on a lightly floured surface. Place dough on a 14-inch pizza pan or baking sheet coated with cooking spray.
2 teaspoons olive oil Heat oil in a large nonstick skillet over medium-high heat. Remove casing from sausage. Add sausage to pan; cook 2 minutes, stirring to crumble. Add mushrooms, bell peppers, onion, crushed red pepper, and garlic; sauté 4 minutes, stirring occasionally.
1 (4-ounce) turkey Italian sausage link Spread sauce over dough, leaving a 1-inch border. Arrange cheese evenly over sauce. Arrange turkey mixture evenly over cheese. Bake at 500° for 15 minutes or until crust and cheese are browned. Cut into 12 wedges.
1 cup thinly sliced red bell pepper
1 cup thinly sliced orange bell pepper
1 cup thinly sliced onion
1/4 teaspoon crushed red pepper
3 garlic cloves, thinly sliced
3/4 cup lower-sodium marinara sauce (such as McCutcheon's)
4 ounces fresh mozzarella cheese, thinly sliced

Breakfast Pizza

Click here





IngredientsDirections
12 ounces refrigerated fresh pizza dough Place a pizza stone or heavy baking sheet in oven. Preheat oven to 500° (keep pizza stone or baking sheet in oven as it preheats).
1 teaspoon olive oil Let dough stand at room temperature for 30 minutes.
2.5 ounces part-skim mozzarella cheese, shredded (about 2/3 cup) Roll dough into a 14-inch circle on a floured surface; pierce entire surface liberally with a fork. Carefully remove pizza stone from oven. Arrange dough on pizza stone. Brush oil over dough. Top evenly with mozzarella cheese and bacon. Crack eggs over top of pizza; sprinkle evenly with Parmesan cheese. Bake at 500° for 8 minutes or until crust is browned and crisp. Sprinkle with chives and parsley. Cut into 8 slices.
3 center-cut bacon slices, cooked and crumbled
4 large eggs
1 tablespoon grated Parmesan cheese
2 tablespoons minced fresh chives
2 tablespoons minced fresh flat-leaf parsley
